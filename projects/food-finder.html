Overview

Food Finder is a smart shelf insert that tracks real-time inventory in community food pantries using load-cell sensors and a connected database. Our goal was to make an affordable, modular, and easy-to-deploy solution.

My Role

I led the mechanical design: CAD modeling, prototyping, rapid iteration, hardware integration, and final optimization. I also supported electronics mounting and system-level testing.

Technical Features

  • Load-cell weight sensing with high-resolution ADC
  • Drop-in modular shelving insert
  • Web-connected logging + database display
  • Mechanical design optimized for manufacturability

Outcome

Food Finder won Best in Section at the Fall 2024 Engineering Design Expo (selected from 89 teams). The system is being prepared for future deployment in local community pantries.
