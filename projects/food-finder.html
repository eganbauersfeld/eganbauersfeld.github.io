Food Finder — Smart Shelf System

Project Overview

Food Finder is a smart shelf insert that tracks real-time inventory in community food pantries using load-cell sensors and a connected database. Our goal was to make an affordable, modular, easy-to-deploy solution.

Image 1: System Overview

My Role

I led the mechanical design: CAD modeling, prototyping, load-cell mounting, and manufacturability optimization. I was responsible for ensuring the system could be easily assembled and deployed in real-world food pantry environments.

Image 2: Prototype

Technical Features

  • High-resolution weight measurement
  • Modular slot-based system
  • WiFi-enabled cloud logging
  • Optimized structure for manufacturability
Image 3: Technical Details

Outcome

Awarded Best in Section at the Fall 2024 Engineering Design Expo. The project successfully demonstrated the viability of affordable IoT solutions for community resource management.
