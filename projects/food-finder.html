Food Finder — Smart Shelf System

Overview

Food Finder is a smart shelf insert that tracks real-time inventory in community food pantries using load-cell sensors and a connected database. Our goal was to make an affordable, modular, easy-to-deploy solution.

System Overview

The insert sits beneath pantry items and continuously measures weight changes to determine real-time stock levels.

My Role

I led the mechanical design: CAD modeling, prototyping, load-cell mounting, and manufacturability optimization.

Technical Features

  • High-resolution weight measurement
  • Modular slot-based system
  • WiFi-enabled cloud logging
  • Optimized structure for manufacturability

Outcome

Awarded Best in Section at the Fall 2024 Engineering Design Expo.
